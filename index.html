"Early life at Pduam"

In loving memory of our late Er. Subhomoy Dey Sir, we fondly remember him as a truly great teacher who left an indelible mark on our lives. His presence was like a ray of sunshine, always illuminating the classroom with his wisdom, guidance, and infectious humor. Er. Subhomoy Dey Sir had a remarkable ability to engage and captivate his students. His teaching methods were not confined to textbooks; he effortlessly wove real-life examples and anecdotes into his lessons, making the subject matter relatable and interesting. His dedication to education went far beyond the curriculum, as he encouraged us to think critically, ask questions, and explore the world around us. One of the most endearing qualities of Er. Subhomoy Dey Sir was his sense of humor. He had an uncanny knack for injecting laughter and joy into the classroom. With his quick wit and amusing anecdotes, he effortlessly lightened the atmosphere, creating a positive and conducive learning environment. His jokes were never meant to demean or belittle anyone; they were simply his way of fostering a sense of camaraderie among his students. Beyond his role as a teacher, Er. Subhomoy Dey Sir was a mentor and a friend. He genuinely cared about the well-being and success of each of his students, going above and beyond to offer guidance and support. Many of us cherish the memories of the times he took the extra effort to provide individual attention, patiently explaining concepts until we understood them. Er. Subhomoy Dey's Sir impact on our lives extends far beyond the classroom. His teachings instilled in us a lifelong love for learning, critical thinking, and embracing challenges. His humor and kindness touched our hearts and left an indelible impression on our souls. As we remember Er. Subhomoy Dey Sir, we are grateful for the privilege of having been his students. His passion for education, his vibrant personality, and his genuine care for his students will forever remain in our hearts. Though he may no longer be with us, his teachings and his spirit continue to inspire us to strive for excellence, find joy in learning, and cherish the bonds we form with him.
